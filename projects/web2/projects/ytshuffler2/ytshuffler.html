YouTube Playlist Shuffler

The function of this tool is pretty self-explanatory. You provide a YouTube playlist URL, and it will pick a random video, and play it. I can't remember when I first had this idea, but I decided to make it one day when my iPod died, and was forced to hastily put together a YouTube playlist of songs I like. The mobile app doesn't provide a shuffling freature (as of time of writing), so I made my own. It was only after making this that I discovered that the new YouTube desktop layout has a shuffle button.

How to use:
1) Copy a YouTube playlist URL. The playlist must not be private, and must actually exist. You can use unlisted playlists.
2) Paste it in to the box below.
3) Ckeck the Autoplay checkbox if you want the video to start straight away.
4) Press "Go!" to shuffle the playlist, and play a song.
5) Refrest the page, or press the "Shuffle!" button to get a new song. It's random, so you may get the same song twice (or more) in a row.
Enjoy the randomness!
