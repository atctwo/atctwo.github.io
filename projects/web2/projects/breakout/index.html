Breakout

One night I was bored, and wanted to do something. Why not play Breakout? That game with the ball and coloured blocks that I realy like. I was on my phone, so I searched Google Play for a Breakout clone, and I could not find one that was as faithful to the original as I would have liked. So, I made my own clone. This is it. I made it in GameMaker: Studio, and for convenience, I exported it as HTML5 so that I could play it online, but I originally developed it as an app. So here it is; have fun!
