Something potentially useful!
In my place of residence, the only way to get the attention of someone who is on a different floor than you is to shout to them. Someone suggested using the net send command avaliable on MS-DOS to contact someone, since they were always on their computer, but the computer was running Windows 10. So I decided to make a thing based on net send, but instead of a computer sending the message, a phone sends the message.

So here is the project: it comes in two parts. There is the client, which lies resident in memory on a computer, and waits for messages from the server which exists in the form of an app. Not mush else to it, apart from the fact that it uses UDP, so the devices must be on the same network. Oh, and the sender can prompt the recipient to reply, should they choose.
